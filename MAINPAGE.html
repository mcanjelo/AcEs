What is AcEs?


✓ Academic Estimator
✓ Webification of Index Cards
✓ For professors and students


With AcEs, professors would be able to :

✓ sign in

✓ know the personal info (including a picture) of their students

✓ know the grades of their students in the pre-requisite classes of the course

✓ give a feedback on a student's performance

✓ know the schedules of their students for scheduling make-up classes

With AcEs, students would be able to :

✓ sign in

✓ edit and update their personal info and picture

✓ enter their grades in the previous terms

✓ enter their class schedule for the term
